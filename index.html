Simulator for demonstration of Kruskal Count

Start from any of the cards at positions #1 to #10 and turn it over. Note the numerical value(aces and the face cards take the numerical value of 1) of the newly upturned card. Count ahead to that many positions and turn over the card you land on. Repeat the same for all of the upturned cards and if the math is on your side, you will end up at the predicted card!
